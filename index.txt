---

## One piece of advice

Avoid trying to learn **every new framework**. Frameworks come and go, but the underlying concepts—LLMs, tools, planning, memory, RAG, MCP, evaluation, and production engineering—remain valuable. Once you understand those deeply, picking up new frameworks becomes much easier.

If you consistently follow this roadmap for three months while building the suggested projects, you'll be well positioned for **AI Engineer** or **Agentic AI Developer** roles and have a portfolio that demonstrates practical, production-oriented skills rather than just tutorial knowledge.
